UNA ANG GINOO – GOD FIRST IN EVERYTHING WE DO

“BUT SEEK FIRST THE KINGDOM OF GOD AND HIS RIGHTEOUSNESS, AND ALL THESE THINGS WILL BE GIVEN TO YOU AS WELL.” — MATTHEW 6:33

For me, UNA ANG GINOO (GOD FIRST) means putting God first has always been a reminder that every step I take should begin with faith. Whether I am studying, working on school projects, or using technology, I believe that God guides my thoughts and actions. When I allow Him to lead, I feel more grounded, more responsible, and more mindful of the choices I make.

Technology is a powerful gift that can connect, inform, and inspire. As a student and as a person guided by values, I believe it is my responsibility to use digital tools wisely. Every post, message, or share reflects who I am, so I choose to act with honesty, kindness, and respect online.

I believe that technology, when guided by faith, can become a tool to inspire, uplift, and spread positivity. Just as our school encourages us to put God first, we can also use social media, websites, and digital platforms responsibly, sharing messages of hope, kindness, and moral values. By reflecting God’s love in our actions, we not only honor Him but also set an example for others, creating a community of respect, honesty, and care.

In our city, our school, and in every step we take, we put God first. Every decision, every action, and every thought begins with seeking His guidance. When we prioritize God, integrity and responsibility naturally follow both in life and online.

SURROUNDED BY GOD’S WORD, WE ARE REMINDED DAILY THAT FAITH LEADS, INSPIRES, AND STRENGTHENS US.

Guided by faith in God, they offered not only their blood but also their hearts in service to others. This act of compassion shows that through God’s love, even small actions can create a great impact in saving lives. In giving selflessly, they became instruments of God’s grace, hope, and healing to those in need.

IN A WORLD FULL OF SCREENS, LET YOUR WORDS SHINE WITH KINDNESS.

"SPREAD WISDOM AND UNDERSTANDING, FOR KNOWLEDGE GIVEN WITH A KIND HEART CAN CHANGE LIVES.” – PROVERBS 4:7

Spending time online can be fun and productive, but it’s important to remember that your health comes first. Take regular breaks from screens, stretch, and breathe. Prioritize sleep and real-life connections to keep your mind and body balanced.

Your mental peace is more valuable than the latest trend or viral content. Step away from social media when you feel stressed, and engage in activities that refresh your mind and spirit.

Every word you type online has an impact. Choosing kindness over criticism can make someone’s day better and create a more positive digital environment. Avoid engaging in cyberbullying, and think before you comment or share.

Social media is a space to connect, not to hurt. Respect others’ opinions, celebrate their successes, and offer encouragement. A single positive message can create a ripple of goodwill.

Every reaction you give online shapes someone’s day—choose the one that spreads kindness.

Spread positivity with every click. The internet needs more voices that heal, not hurt.

React with kindness, because you never know what someone is going through behind the screen.

PRACTICE DIGITAL KINDNESS

Kindness is the foundation of healthy online communication. Choose respectful, thoughtful words to create a safer and more supportive digital space.

BE HONEST IN YOUR DIGITAL CREATIONS

Online content should reflect truth, integrity, and responsibility. Do not copy, mislead, or spread false information—create with honesty and purpose.

RESPECT OTHERS IN EVERY ONLINE INTERACTION

Every comment you post affects real people with real emotions. Treat others with the same dignity and respect you expect in return.

THINK BEFORE YOU CLICK OR POST

Pause and evaluate your message before sending it. A responsible digital citizen considers accuracy, empathy, and respect in every action.
